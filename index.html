The Natural Path For You
The Natural Path For You is a career path in which you can be your Natural Self and Capitalize on your Strengths!
Consultation: Children and Adults
Inspired by the Japanese concept “Ikigai”
Follow us on: www.facebook.com/ thenaturalpath4you
Career is not a one-time decision but a series of small & big decisions with respect to career that we make at various points of time in our life – not only a job that you decide to take or not, but also the certificate courses you decide to do, a skill that you choose to learn, the projects that you are involved in, the people you choose to interact with and so on.
For a fulfilling career and a harmonious life, it is important that these decisions are made consciously with awareness about one’s own self - natural inclinations, preferences and motivations. Life is a journey of exploration, in finding ourselves and our place in this world. Children, in particular, should keep actively and widely exploring their interests to understand their own self!
