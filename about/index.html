What We Do
TNP4U offers customized career counseling services using a practical and intuitive approach for both children (13+) and adults (18+) to help discover/design "THE NATURAL PATH FOR YOU" - a career path in which you can be your natural self & capitalize on your strengths! This could mean discovering your own potential, strengths, a direction, a type of profession or a niche, important career elements and using that information to consciously decide on what to do next.
